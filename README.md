# 📊 Customer Retention Analysis (SQL Project)

## 🎯 Objective

Analyze customer purchase behavior to identify retention patterns and reasons for churn.

---

## 📌 Business Problem

Many customers make only one purchase and do not return.
The goal is to measure retention and identify improvement opportunities.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* SQL (CTE, JOIN, Aggregation)

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Customers table
* Orders table (includes category, amount, discount, payment method)

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis

* Identified first and second purchase of customers
* Calculated customer retention rate
* Analyzed retention by product category
* Measured time between purchases

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Books category has the lowest retention rate
* Customers take X days on average to make a second purchase
* Majority of customers are one-time buyers

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Improve product recommendations
* Offer targeted discounts
* Introduce bundling strategies
